About the Game

Tips

You can press "P" to pause the gameplay and Space to make the game run faster.

Intro

The game comes with a nice retro-style menu and arcade background music

Also, it has a variety of options that can be turned on and off (black is off, white is on)

Marty, the snake is an albino snake that wants to get as many fruits as he can in order to get a nice color.

This is just one of the many colors Marty can get, depending on the amount of fruits that he eats and their color.

The game also has a "borderless" game option.

This version is just for Windows. I might release an Linux & Android version too, depending on whether I'll hve the time or not.
